All About Me…

Hello world, my name is Alvida Carter and I’ve had a long road in my attempts to partake within this journey of Wed Development. I am currently working for Comcast’s Business Services Department as a Web Content Editor and I couldn’t be more thrilled to be attending this NYCDA Web Development class. 

In design school I took numerous courses in web design, but at the time of my graduation the market shifted, so sad to say I took the first opportunity that came my way. Thankfully, years later the skies parted and a rainbow appeared and so here I am having a blast doing what I truly love. I am confident that this course will amplify my contributions to the projects set before me. 

So look out world!
